## Overview

The Supersaurus bone interactive at Thanksgiving Point's Museum of Ancient Life invites visitors to guess which dinosaur the Supersaurus is related to based solely on scapula bone graphics. Guests can place the Supersaurus' scapula in 3 slots corresponding to 3 different dinosaur scapulae. When they're ready to check their answer, they press a button and an LED light strip installed behind the frosted acrylic slots lights up red or green, giving them immediate feedback on their guess.

I thought this would be a great opportunity to challenge myself to design a system that accomplished the requirements of the interactive without using a microcontroller, which led me into a fantastic journey learning about 555 timers, MOSFETs, PCB design and manufacturing, and more. All my findings and documentation will live here, including schematics and links to datasheets where relevant.
